FAQ



What types of clothing do you offer?

We offer a wide range of clothing for men, women, and children, including t-shirts, blouses, pants, dresses, jackets, sweaters, and more.

Do you have a size chart?

Yes, we have a size chart available on our website to help you find the right size for your body. Please note that sizing may vary depending on the brand and style of clothing.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy on all items that are unworn, unwashed, and in their original condition with tags still attached. We do not offer returns on final sale items.

How can I pay for my purchase?

We accept all major credit cards, PayPal, and Apple Pay. In-store, we also accept cash and debit cards.

How long does shipping take?

Shipping times vary depending on your location and the shipping method you choose. Standard shipping typically takes 3-5 business days, while expedited shipping can take 1-2 business days.

Can I track my order?

Yes, we provide a tracking number for all orders once they have been shipped. You can track your order on our website or through the shipping carrier's website.

Do you offer gift cards?

Yes, we offer physical and digital gift cards in a range of denominations. They make a great gift for any occasion.

Can I pick up my order in-store?

Yes, we offer free in-store pickup for all orders. You can select this option at checkout and we will email you when your order is ready for pickup.

Do you offer international shipping?

Yes, we offer international shipping to select countries. Please note that shipping rates and times may vary depending on your location.

How can I contact customer service?

You can contact our customer service team by email, phone, or live chat. Our contact information is available on our website.
